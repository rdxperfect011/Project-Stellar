Explore Jammu

Jammu is known as the City of Temples, and many popular attractions are easily accessible from our property.

Raghunath Temple

One of North India's most important temple complexes dedicated to Lord Rama.

Bahu Fort

A historic fort overlooking the Tawi River and home to the revered Bawe Wali Mata Temple.

Bagh-e-Bahu

Beautiful terraced gardens offering scenic views of Jammu city.

Ranbireshwar Temple

A prominent and historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Mubarak Mandi Palace

The majestic former palace of the Maharaja of Jammu and Kashmir.

Amar Mahal & Peer Kho

The grand Amar Mahal Palace Museum and the ancient Peer Kho Cave Temple.

Many visitors also use Jammu as a gateway to the famous Vaishno Devi Shrine. Community travel recommendations frequently include Raghunath Temple, Bahu Fort, Amar Mahal, and Mubarak Mandi among the top places to explore in Jammu.
